Laurel

Laurel’s makeup career began with a love for making people feel beautiful. At an early age, Laurel realized she had a natural gift for doing makeup and pursued her dream to make it a career. She graduated from the Academy of Makeup Arts in Nashville, Tennessee and became a Professional Makeup Artist. Laurel specializes in Airbrush makeup and looks such as: natural beauty, dramatic smoky eye, glamorous, period looks, and fashion. Whether working with a professional model on a photo shoot, or a bride on her special day, Laurel's unique ability to connect with her client makes her

the perfect choice.
